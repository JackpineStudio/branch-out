About Us

We love delicious cookies that everyone can enjoy; no matter what what your life choices or allergens are. Our cookies and squares are allergen-free, all plant-based, and organic wherever possible. The best part? They’re delivered right to your door when you need them.

All our baked goods are transported by bicycle, so as to reduce our carbon footprint. Why not order some delicious cookies from Ottawa’s most sustainable bakery for your next party or event?
